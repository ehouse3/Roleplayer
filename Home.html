ROLEPLAYER

The 2D tabletop simulator for tabletop role playing games

A webdev project, by Euan House

Controls:

Hold LMB on token to drag

Hold LMB on board and drag to select multiple tokens

Hold RMB and drag to pan around the board

Features:

Token information tab w/ selected token's stats

Zoom functionality including a zoom bar, scroll bars & positional based cursor zooming

Additions to come:

Settings menu

Changable token stats
